    <h1>Nikhil Kumar</h1>
    <p>Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in Public Policy (Economics track) at Harvard University. My research focuses on human capital and socioeconomic mobility in low- and middle-income countries.</p>

    <p>Before starting my graduate studies, I have worked as an RA at <a href="https://www.hks.harvard.edu/centers/cid">CID</a>, <a href="https://www.bu.edu/econ/">BU</a> and <a href="https://www.nber.org/">NBER</a> in the Boston-Cambridge area.</p>
